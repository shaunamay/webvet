Pet Services


Dog Walking

Our experienced dog walkers will provide your dog with the exercise, attention and care they need while you work. Your dog can be walked alone or with a group. Personalised programs are available.

Price Range: €10-€50

Home Visits

Home visits typically involve calling to your house once or twice daily to take care of your pet(s). Ideal for pets that are extremely attached to their home surroundings.

Price Range: €20-€100

Pet Taxi

A pet taxi is a transportation service for your pet. Our service allows your pet(s) to be driven unaccompanied to and from the location of your choice by our fully accredited pet taxi drivers.

Price Range: Varies by Location

Grooming

Our typical professional grooming session consists of your pet being brushed, bathed and dried, as well as trimmed or clipped with high quaality brushes and clipper blades.

Price Range: €25-€80

24 Hour Care

Pets can become ill when we least expect it. We provide an Emergency Vet service 24/7 to assist you in your pet's time of need. Your pet's safety and welfare is our number one priority.

Price Range: On Call Fee €30
